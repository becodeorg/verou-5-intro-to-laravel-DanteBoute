Sherlock

"Sherlock" is a modern adaptation of Sir Arthur Conan Doyle's legendary detective stories, featuring the brilliant consulting detective Sherlock Holmes and his loyal companion Dr. John Watson. Set in contemporary London, the series follows Holmes as he uses his unparalleled deductive skills to solve complex crimes, navigate personal challenges, and unravel mysteries that keep viewers on the edge of their seats.

Main Cast

  • Benedict Cumberbatch as Sherlock Holmes
  • Martin Freeman as Dr. John Watson

Episodes

"Sherlock" consists of four seasons, each with a handful of feature-length episodes. The series captivates audiences with its clever storytelling, modern twists on classic cases, and the dynamic relationship between Sherlock and Watson.
